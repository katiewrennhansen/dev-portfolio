USModernist

This redesign was completed for USModernist, and its sister site NCModernist, which together make up the largest archive of modernist architecture in the nation today. This 501C3 nonprofit and educational archive aims to document, preserve and promote modernist architecture around the country. The goal for this redesign was to build out an improved design for the site that achieved a more modern look and feel that more closely aligned with the goals of the non-profit.

Phase 2:

I am currently building an ArcGIS database for NCModernist to store all of the data that has been gathered over the years. Once this database is complete, I will be adding a custom map to the home page along with other improved site functionality.
