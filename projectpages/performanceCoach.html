The Performance Coach

Purpose:

This website was built for a personal trainer to help promote his sport and athletic training business. Users are able to find information regarding his upcoming services, training policies and training philosophy. He also wanted to display a calendar that would allow clients to view his availability, as well as have a way for clients to purchase his services or contact him directly.

Process:

For this project, I built a static site using HTML, CSS and Javascript loosely based on designs provided by a web designer who had previously worked with the client. The calendar component was created using Google Calendar and later embedded on the site so that the trainer would have full autonomy over adding and updating his availability. Initially, the payment page contained a dropdown holding all of his available services. Clients could select a service after which they would be taken to his PayPal page for that service. This functionality was later removed as the client decided that he would rather people contact him directly before purchasing a service. The site uses responsive design and functions well on all screen sizes.
