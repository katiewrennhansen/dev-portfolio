About

I believe that each individual should have the opportunity to express their unique ideas. It is my mission to contribute to a world where these ideas are accessible to everyone by showcasing them as elegant, yet functional web pages. I am here to help build a platform through which your ideas can be shared with the world.

I gravitated towards web development after realizing its perfect union of problem solving and visual creativity. Those were two skills I rarely exercised in my previous career, and I enjoyed the challenge of the task. Nothing is more satisfying than ‘ah-ha’ moment you get after solving a complex problem, or the satisfaction of bringing an idea to life.

I am a self taught developer, so there is still much to learn, but I enjoy every day spent learning new skills and building projects. When I am not coding or working on projects, you can find me running, baking, or traveling to all corners of the world.
